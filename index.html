Key Projects



  • Title: RABBITECH: Automatic Feeding and Monitoring System for Rabbitry

    Mico and the research group developed Rabbitech, a rabbit care technology that enhances rabbit care through its app, efficiently managing feeding schedules and water levels. The application was developed using C++ programming language, VS Code, and Flutter IDE, ensuring efficient performance. The system integrates Arduino IDE, Arduino UNO, NodeMCU, ultrasonic sensors, load cells, RTC, and DHT22 sensors to maintain optimal conditions and enable precise food dispensing. Its compact design allows for easy installation in cages.

    Major Responsibilities:

    Programming and Testing

    Created a mobile application.

    Writing of technical documents

    Technologies: C++, VSCode, Flutter, Arduino IDE, & NodeMCU

    Other technologies such as hardware: Arduino UNO, NodeMCU, DHT22 Sensor, Geared Motor, Load Cell Amplifier, Ultrasonic Sensor, RTC Module, Ultrasonic Module, and Servo Motor


  • Title: Monitoring System using VBA Macro for Enhancing the Safety of Visitors in Tanauan City College

    Mico and the research group developed a monitoring system using VBA macro can significantly enhance the safety of visitors in Tanauan City College. VBA, a visual basic for applications programming language, can be utilized to create a macro that automatically tracks and records visitors' information, including their names, contact details, and the purpose of their visit. This information can then be used to create a comprehensive database that can be accessed by authorized personnel.

    Major Responsibilities:

    Programming and Testing

    Writing of technical documents

    Technologies: Excel & Visual Basic for Applications - VBA Programming
